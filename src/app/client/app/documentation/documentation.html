App Description

There are two views for the drug networks of both NCI60 and CTRP, Exemplar and Full Network.
  Exemplar displays the network of exemplar drugs representative of their corresponding drug communities.
  Full Network displays the exemplar drugs as well as all the corresponding drug communities.


Maneuvering the App

There are two different ways to view the drug networks:
  1) Click and drag anywhere in the view to move left, right, up or down.
  2) Use the arrows on the toolbar on the left to move.

  

To zoom into the app, you can either:
  1) Scroll using your mouse scroll wheel - scrolling up zooms in, scrolling down zooms out.
  2) Use the magnifying glasses on the toolbar on the left.

  


Similarity Scores

Select an edge between two nodes (drugs) to see the similarity scores based on basic drug properties (perturbation, similarity, and physical structure).
The contributions are displayed in a pie chart of all three categories.
 →    → 

Hover over the sections of the pie chart to view the numerical similarity scores.




Drug Communities

Select a node (drug) to view its corresponding drug community.

Drug Properties

After selecting a node to view its drug community, select a node within the community to view its individual properties and PubChem link.
 →    → 

The drug properties shown include chemical names, the molecular formula, molecular weight, and link to its PubChem page.
